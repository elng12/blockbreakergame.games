Google Block Breaker

Rediscover the iconic arcade classic, now available to play instantly on any device.

How to Play

The goal is simple, but the challenge is timeless. Use your mouse or finger to move the paddle at the bottom of the screen. Your objective is to bounce the ball to clear every block without letting it fall past your paddle.

A Timeless Classic Reborn

Originally released in 1976 by Atari, Breakout became one of the most famous arcade games of all time. Its simple yet addictive gameplay loop has inspired countless variations, including a famous Easter egg hidden by Google in its image search. This version brings you that pure, focused experience directly in your browser.

Game Features

  • Classic Gameplay: Pure, retro arcade action just as you remember it.
  • Simple Controls: Intuitive mouse or touch controls make it easy to pick up and play.
  • Fully Responsive: Enjoy a seamless experience on your desktop, tablet, or mobile phone.
  • Distraction-Free: Just the game. No ads, no pop-ups, just block-breaking fun.
